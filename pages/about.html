Our Story

Welcome to Brew Haven, where passion meets perfection in every cup. Founded in 2024, our journey began with a simple dream, to create a cozy corner where coffee enthusiasts could gather, unwind, and savor the finest brews in town. Nestled in the heart of Oaxaca, Brew Haven is more than just a coffee shop; it's a sanctuary for those who appreciate the art of coffee-making. Our mission is to deliver an exceptional coffee experience through carefully sourced beans, expert brewing techniques, and a warm, inviting atmosphere. At Brew Haven, every cup is crafted with love and dedication, ensuring that each sip is a moment of pure delight.

Our Coffee

We believe that great coffee starts with great beans. That's why we source our coffee from ethically grown, sustainable farms around the world. Each batch is carefully roasted to perfection, highlighting the unique flavors and aromas of each origin. Our baristas are trained to bring out the best in every bean, whether you prefer a bold espresso or a smooth, creamy latte.
